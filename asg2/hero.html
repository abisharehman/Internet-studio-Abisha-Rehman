Selena Gomez: A business woman

Selena Gomez started Rare Beauty to challenge the makeup space by creating a brand centered on mental health and self-acceptance instead of the usual pressures to look a certain way. A major focus of the brand is its strong connection to mental health advocacy. A percentage of all Rare Beauty sales are donated to the Rare Impact Fund, which works to increase access to mental health services for underserved communities. This made rare beauty stand out from all other brands.
