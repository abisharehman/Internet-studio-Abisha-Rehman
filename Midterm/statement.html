Statement

When choosing visuals, size, and color palette. I wanted to choose the colors used for the movie and game because I wanted to show the aesthetic of the movie. I also wanted to choose the best images to use that really showed what I was talking about when referencing scenes from the movie so I used movie clips. I also wanted the images to not be so big that it would take over the entire screen, because then it would make the whole layout overwhelming.

When it came to fonts I also wanted to use something simple, at first I wondered if I should use video game aesthetic fonts, but I wanted the midterm to be easy to read. I think having a more simple layout when it comes to fonts makes the assignment look clean and more modern, compared to other fonts that can make things chaotic and hard to read.
