Things To Do

Visit Hero's Duty

Hero duty is a game in the universe where the mission is to eliminate deadly cy bugs. Cy bugs are robot alien invaders and the team's job is to take them out. The main goal of the game is to fight your way to the top of a building to get a medal called the hero medal.

Explore Diet Cola Mountain

Diet cola mountain is the mountain where one of the characters named Vanellope in the movie lived. The area is shaped like a volcano and was an unfinished bonus level in the game Sugar rush. Here others can come and hangout like Vanellope did in the movie.

Build your car in Sugar Rush

In the movie Ralph and Vanellope made their own car because she did not have one. There was an entire section in the sugar rush game where you could customize, build, and finish your own race car.
