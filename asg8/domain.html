The Domain Name You Like Best and Why

The name the I chose for my project will be "Bookishjewels.website", I believe it’s simple, memorable, and not too complicated. When choosing a domain name, I think it’s best to pick something that’s easy to recall and clearly represents the project. When I talked about the other domain name options the group and I decided that the other names most likely had nothing to do with what my final project wanted to be.
